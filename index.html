About Me

After many years in the IT industry as a network engineer, I have decided to try something different. I have spent many hours troubleshooting, designing, and supporting IT infrastructure, but now I have chosen a field where I can be creative as well as remaining technical.

Growing up in Chicago in the 80's, I was like any other kid at the that time. I was fascinated by video games like Atari, Nintendo, and Sega. I also wondered what it would be like to be a part of the team that designs the games. Now, at the peak of my career, I have decided to dive in and find out what web design and development is all about
